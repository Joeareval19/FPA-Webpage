About VCP

We are a specialized consulting firm dedicated to modernizing financial infrastructure for the hospitality industry.

Our Story

Vegas Consulting Partners (VCP) was founded on the principle that the hospitality industry deserves better, more efficient, and more intelligent financial tools. We saw a gap between the cutting-edge of technology and the day-to-day operations of hotels, casinos, and resorts. Our mission is to bridge that gap.

Our team is comprised of industry veterans, financial experts, and technology pioneers. We leverage the power of the full Microsoft suite ecosystem and state-of-the-art AI solutions to deliver battle-tested, YC Combinator-backed tools directly to your business.

We don't just provide software; we provide solutions. We work with you to understand your unique challenges and opportunities, and then we build a roadmap to success. We are your partners in innovation.
